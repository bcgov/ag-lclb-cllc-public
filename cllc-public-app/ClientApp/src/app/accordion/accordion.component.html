A cannabis retail store licence allows you to sell non-medical cannabis in British Columbia.

Below is a step-by-step guide to getting your cannabis retail licence and selling your products.

 

 | 
1 Register your business or organization
  • You need to have a business or organization that is registered in B.C.
  • Your business/organization must be registered to collect PST.
  • Your business/organization must have a Business Number (9-digit number issued by the Canada Revenue Agency)
 2 Create a Business BCeID
  • Your business/organization will need a Business BCeID to log in.
  • Your business/organization can have only one Business BCeID, but you should create separate accounts for each person using it.
 3Find a location for your store
  • Contact the local government or First Nation to make sure they are accepting applications for cannabis stores in their community.
  • Ask about the local requirements for zoning, bylaws and business licences.
  • You must own, lease, or have an agreement to lease the location.

Stores in rural areas have different location requirements.

 4Submit your application

Your application requires:

  • Information about your business/organization.
  • Information about your proposed location.
  • Consent for security clearance and financial integrity checks.
  • An application fee.
  • A declaration of financial, business or family connections to any licensed producers.

You must apply for a separate licence for each store.

 5 Get local government approval
  • Your application must be approved by the local government or First Nation before you can be licensed. Ask them about any local approvals or zoning requirements.
  • Local governments must hold a public consultation for the area around your store.
  • First Nations may follow the local government approval process, or one of their own.
 6 Receive conditional approval

If your application meets local and provincial requirements, you will get conditional approval from the province. Conditional approval means you will receive your licence after passing a final inspection.

Now you can:

  • Secure ownership or a lease of your store.
  • Make sure you meet the terms and conditions (e.g. hours of operations) of your licence.
  • Make changes to your store to match the description you submitted in your application, including:
    • Floor plan
    • Exterior signage
    • Window covering
 7 Pass a store inspection
  • The inspection ensures your store meets provincial regulation.
  • You can schedule an inspection when you are ready.

You cannot have any cannabis in your store at the time of the inspection.

 8 Receive your licence
  • You will receive your licence after passing your inspection and paying a first-year licensing fee.
  • You must display your licence in a prominent place in your store.
 9 Register and train your staff
  • Your staff must be registered with the province before they can work in your store.
  • A provincial training program is currently under development.
  • More information about registration and training will be available soon.
 10 Order cannabis products
  • You can only sell products that you purchase from the Liquor Distribution Branch.
  • You need your licence number to place an order.
 11 Report your sales
  • You need to report your inventory and sales on a regular basis.
