Apply for a cannabis retail store licence

A cannabis retail store licence allows licensees to sell non-medical cannabis and cannabis accessories in British Columbia. To apply for a private cannabis retail store license, you need to have a registered Business BCeID. You will use your BCeID to access the online application service.
