Qualify by November 30

Workers can begin working at licensed cannabis retail stores now, but have until November 30 to apply for and receive their qualification. After that date, all current and future workers must have their qualification to be allowed to work in cannabis retail stores.

Using the BC Services Card

If you have a BC Services Card you must use it to log in to the online qualification page. Learn more about the BC Services Card. Learn more about eligibility requirements to obtain a BC Services Card. (Note: This link will take you to a B.C. Medical Services Plan (MSP) page. The eligibility criteria for the BC Services Card is the same as for MSP.)

Applicants Without a BC Services Card

Applicants who haven’t lived in B.C. for at least six months or who live outside the province must email the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch for a Worker Qualification Application Form.
