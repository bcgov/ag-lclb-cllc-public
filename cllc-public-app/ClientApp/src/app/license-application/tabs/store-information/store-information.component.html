Store Information


Your store name must be approved, and cannot imply you are selling medicinal cannabis

This is required

Words related to medicinal products or similarly sounding words will not be approved such as:

  • Pharmacy / Farmacy
  • Dispensary / Dispenzary
  • Clinic / Klinic
  • Apothecary / Apothekary

Hours of Operation

Cannabis stores can only operate between 9 a.m. and 11 p.m seven days a week. Your licence wil be given these hours of operation limits unless your local government or First Nation places further restrictions on the hours.

Exterior Views & Signage

Upload photos or renderings of your store's proposed exterior signage and all exterior views around the store

Your signage must:

  • Show the name of the store
  • Show all words and images used in the signage
  • Not contain any pictures, images, or symbols that imply that you sell medicinal cannabis. For example, a green cross
  • Not imply an association with another business. This applies to the store name, trademarks and any type of branding
  • Demonstrate that cannabis products are not visible from outside the store
