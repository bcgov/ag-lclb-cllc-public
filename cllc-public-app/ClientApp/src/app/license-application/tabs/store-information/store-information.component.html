Store Information


Your store name must be approved, and cannot imply you are selling medical cannabis

This is required

Words related to medicinal products or similarly sounding words will not be approved such as:

  • Pharmacy / Farmacy
  • Dispensary / Dispenzary
  • Clinic / Klinic
  • Apothecary / Apothekary
  • Drugs / Drug Store / Drug Department

Hours of Operation

Cannabis stores are permitted to operate between 9 a.m. and 11 p.m seven days a week. Your licence will be given these hours of operation unless the local government or Indigenous nation imposes shorter hours.

Your signage and store exterior must:

  • Show the name of the store
  • Show all words and images used in the signage
  • Not contain any pictures, images or symbols that imply that you sell medical cannabis. (for example, a green cross)
  • Not imply an association with a separate business. This applies to the store name, trademarks and any type of branding
  • Demonstrate that cannabis products are not visible from outside the store. (for example, tinted or covered windows)
