Submitting a Site Plan

Upload a site plan of the area surrounding your store. The plan must be clear and legible in PDF format.

Your site plan must reflect what your store will look like at the time of your licence being issued.

Your site plan must show:

  • The location of the building and where it's located on the property
  • The location of all entrances and exits to the store
  • The locations of other buildings and businesses nearby, the entrances to those buildings, and their names or a description of what they are (e.g. gas station, office building)
  • A description of the type of separation proposed for any adjoining businesses
  • Relevant street names nearby
  • Road access to the property, including the locations of any driveways and parking lots

Stores Located Inside a Building with other Businesses

If the store is located inside a building with other businesses (for example, a mall), provide two site plans:

One site plan showing the location of any other buildings and businesses on the same site including:

  • Each building footprint, building name and a list of businesses operating inside the building
  • Relevant street names nearby
  • Road access to the property, including the locations of any driveways and parking lots
