Welcome to Cannabis Retail Store Licensing

A non-medical cannabis retail store licence allows licensees to sell non-medical cannabis and cannabis accessories in British Columbia.

In order to apply for a non-medical cannabis retail store licence, applicants must submit a completed application form and completed business financial integrity form. A separate application and business financial integrity form is required for each proposed establishment.

The Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch (LCRB) will only process complete applications. The checklist accompanying the application form can assist in completing all required documentation.

Please note that applications which are submitted without all required information or documents will not be processed. Applicants will be contacted and provided the opportunity to submit missing information. Once applicants provide all required documents to the LCRB, the application will be submitted for processing.

Inquires related to your application can be directed to cannabis.licensing@gov.bc.ca.
