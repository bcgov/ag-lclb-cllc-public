Applying as {{data.businessType}}

As {{data.businessType}} you must submit:

  • Registered business address
  • Notice of articles
  • A list of all owners, directors, and officers
  • A list of all shareholders with 10% or more of voting shares

Background Checks for {{data.businessType}}

Background checks will be required for all owners, directors, officers, and shareholders with 10% or more of voting shares. You will authorize the province to conduct these checks during the application process.

Register for a Business BCeID

A Business BCeID provides secure access for businesses to access online services.

You can have multiple Business BCeIDs associated to your business. The person submitting the licence application must have their own unique BCeID and be authorized by the business to submit the application.

To begin, visit Register for a Business BCeID

Information about your store

You must submit:

  • The parcel identification number (PID) of the location
  • Proof of ownership or a copy of a fully executed lease that does not expire for at least 12 months from the date of an expected licence approval
  • A floorplan

Floorplans are used to identity the sales area versus the back area.

The floor plan you submit will be used during the final inspection to ensure what you told us about your store matches the real layout. Any renovations or modifications of the layout should be done before submitting the floorplan, as these must match exactly as described.
