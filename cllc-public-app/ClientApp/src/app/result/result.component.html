What you need to prepare

Age Eligibility

  • You are not eligible to apply for a cannabis retail store licence if you are under 19 years old.

Licensed producer of cannabis

You are not eligible for a cannabis retail store licence if you are a federally licensed producer of cannabis.

Registered business

You must have a registered business to apply for a licence. The type of business you register will determine who will need a security clearance check and what business information you need to submit. You can register a business with OneStop, B.C.'s Business Registry. You should also register for a Business BCeID.

As a public corporation, you must submit the corporation's:

  • Name
  • Contact information (mailing address, email, phone number)

Business BCeID

Your business's Business BCeID will be used to log in to the cannabis retail licence application. Your business can register only one legal entity for a Business BCeID. Additional Business BCeID user accounts can be created for people who may access the application on the organization’s behalf. Contact a BCeID Business Profile Manager or a Business Account Manager to request a Business BCeID user account.

Information about your store

Your store must be a self-contained business.

Your property arrangement must be valid for at least 12 months after you would receive your licence.

During the application, you will need to submit the following pieces of information about your store's location:

  • PID (either a nine-digit number or a legal description of your property)
  • Photos/renderings of your store’s exterior and signage
  • A floor plan showing:
    • All doors leading into/out of the sales area
    • All entrances to the store (including back doors)
    • The locations of all fixed shelving and/or display cases
    • The locations of all cash registers
    • Which areas are public and which are for staff only
    • The locations of all storage areas
  • A site plan showing:
    • The location of the building and where it’s located on the property
    • The location of all entrances to the store
    • The locations of other buildings and businesses nearby, the entrances to those buildings, and their names or a description of what they are (e.g. gas station, office building)
    • Relevant street names nearby
    • Road access to the property, including the locations of any driveways and parking spots

Information about your store

Connections to Licensed Producers

Restrictions will be placed on a retail store that has a close business, financial or family connection to a producer. You will be prohibited from selling any of a federal producer's products if:

  • You own 20% or more of a producer's voting shares
  • One of your immediate family members owns 20% or more of a producer's voting shares
  • A producer owns 20% or more of your voting shares
  • Any company, person or group of people owns 20% or more of both your business's and a producer's voting share

When you apply, you must declare any business, financial or family connections that you have to a producer, even if less than 20%.
