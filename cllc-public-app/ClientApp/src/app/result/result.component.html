What you need to prepare

Age Eligibility

  • You are not eligible to apply for a cannabis retail licence if you are under 19 years old

Registered business or organization

You must have a registered business or organization to apply for a licence. The type of business your register will determine who will need a security clearance check and what business informaion you need to submit. You can register a business with BC Registries.

First Nations Land

If the First Nation has a population of less than 2500 people and your proposed location is at least 10km from any other retail cannabis application, you may qualify for a rural cannabis retail licence.

First Nation

Keep communicating with the First Nation about requirements for cannabis stores that are specific to their community. You may be required to get a local business licence and zoning approval from the First Nation to sell cannabis.

The First Nation must approve your cannabis store application. Contact the First Nation to make sure they are accepting applications for cannabis stores and to ask about local requirements. First Nations may choose to follow the local government approval process, or one of their own. You may be required to get a local business licence and zoning approval from the First Nation to sell cannabis.

Information about your store

During the application, you will need to submit the following pieces of information about your store's location.

  • Parcel Identifier Number
  • Floorplan showing:
  • All doors leading into/out of the sales area
    • All entrances to the store (including back doors)
    • The locations of all fixed shelving and/or display cases
    • The locations of all cash registers
    • Which areas are public and which are for staff only
    • The dimensions of all rooms
    • The locations of all storage areas
  • Siteplan showing:
    • The location of your store’s main public entrance
    • The locations of other buildings and businesses nearby, the entrances to those buildings, and their names or a description of what they are (e.g. gas station, office building)
    • Relevant street names nearby
  • Photos or renderings of your store's exterior and signage

Make sure that any lease or property arrangement will be valid for at least 12 months after you would receive a licence.

You do not have to commit to buying or leasing a location before you submit an application. However, you must have an arrangement that will allow you to commit to a location if you are approved. Once your licence is conditially approved, you must gain ownership, a lease or have an acceptable property arrangement in place (such as a sublease or a property in the name of one of your business partners).

Make sure that any lease or property arrangement will be valid for at least 12 months after you would receive a licence.

Connection to Licensed Producer or Cultivator

Restrictions will be placed on a retail store that has a connection to a licenced producer or processor. If there’s a close association with the producer or processor, the retailer will be prohibited from selling any of their products. The criteria to determine this connection is currently being created.
