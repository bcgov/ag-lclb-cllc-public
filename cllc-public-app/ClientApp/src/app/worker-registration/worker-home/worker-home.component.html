Get a BC Services Card

B.C. residents who have lived in the province for at least six months must use a BC Services Card to log in to the Cannabis Worker Registry. Learn how to get a card.

Applicants Without a BC Services Card

Applicants who haven’t lived in B.C. for at least six months or who live outside the province must email the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch for a Worker Registration Application Form.
