ORGANIZATION STRUCTURE

All licensees and applicants must maintain a complete list of key personnel and businesses associated with their organization.

All associates of the applicant are required to submit an associate organization assessment form. The LCRB may identify additional associates during processing of the application, and those associates may be required to submit additional documentation as part of the application process.

Please visit the Organization Information page to complete this information.

Once the application is submitted, provide associates with a link to the Associates forms page, which contains the instructions and required forms for associates.

ORGANIZATION STRUCTURE

Licensees are required to maintain a complete and current list of key personnel and businesses associated with their organization.

You are required to notify the LCRB of any changes to your organization’s structure within ten business days of the change being made.

Please visit the Organization Information page to make changes to your organization’s structure.

Fees will be calculated based on the type of change reported and the number of licences that will be impacted.
