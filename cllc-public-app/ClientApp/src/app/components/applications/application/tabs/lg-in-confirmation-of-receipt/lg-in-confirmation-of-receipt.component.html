Occupant Load is the maximum number of people (patrons plus staff) permitted in a service area. The occupant load must be stamped or written, dated and signed on the floor plan by the appropriate authority.

If you will not provide the occupant load for the applicant, you must provide a letter confirming you do not issue occupant load. The Branch will accept an occupant load calculation from a professional architect or engineer.

A licence cannot be issued without final occupant load.
