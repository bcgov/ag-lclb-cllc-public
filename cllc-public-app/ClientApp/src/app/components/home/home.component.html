Apply for a liquor or cannabis licence

Log in below to access the Liquor and Cannabis Licensing portal where you can apply for a cannabis retail store license, cannabis marketing licence or liquor-related licence, manage changes to your account, or renew and pay application fees easily from your desktop computer, phone or tablet. Please note, you must have a Business BCeID to use the online application service.

Currently, only catering and wine store liquor licences can be managed through the portal, but more liquor licence types will be added soon. Once a liquor licence type is available online, liquor licensees and applicants can use their Business BCeID to login.

The Liquor and Cannabis Licensing portal is closed for maintenance. It will be available again Monday, April 20th. During this time, you will not be able to log in to the site.
